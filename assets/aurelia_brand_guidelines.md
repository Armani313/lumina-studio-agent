# Aurelia — Brand Guidelines (sample, fictional brand)

> Internal brand book for **Aurelia**, a minimalist premium skincare label. Used as the
> knowledge base for the Lumina Studio Agent's Brand RAG (Vertex AI Search) grounding.

## Brand essence
Aurelia is clean, clinical, and calm. We sell efficacy without noise. Every asset should feel
like a quiet, premium dermatology lab — never loud, never playful, never "beauty-influencer".

## Tone of voice
- **Do:** precise, understated, science-backed, reassuring. Short declarative sentences.
- **Don't:** hype words ("miracle", "game-changer"), exclamation marks, emoji, slang.
- Reading level: confident and adult. Sound like a formulator, not a marketer.

## Color palette (authoritative hex)
- Bone White `#F4F1EC` (primary background)
- Warm Travertine `#D8CDBD` (surfaces/props)
- Clay Taupe `#B8A892` (secondary)
- Ink Charcoal `#2B2A28` (text/labels)
- Sage Accent `#9CA98C` (sparingly, botanical cues only)
- **Forbidden:** pure black `#000000`, pure white `#FFFFFF`, any saturated/neon color, gold/metallic gradients.

## Typography
- Headlines: a light grotesque sans (think clean, wide tracking). Lowercase or sentence case.
- Body: a neutral humanist sans. Never script, never serif display.
- Labels on packaging show only the wordmark "AURELIA" — no secondary slogans on the bottle.

## Imagery direction
- Photoreal product-in-environment on natural stone (travertine), linen, or matte plaster.
- Soft, diffused natural daylight; gentle shadows; shallow depth of field.
- Props: eucalyptus, cotton, raw stone. Sparingly.
- **Avoid:** harsh studio flash, busy backgrounds, visible text overlays, watermarks, people-heavy scenes.

## Product fidelity rules
- The bottle is **frosted glass with a dropper**; matte ivory label; the wordmark "AURELIA".
- Keep the bottle silhouette and label legible and singular. No invented sub-brands or garbled text.

## Channels
- Instagram feed 4:5, Stories 9:16, Amazon 1:1, web hero 16:9.
- Copy stays fragrance-free / clinical; always note "fragrance-free" where relevant.

## Claims & compliance
- Allowed: "brightening", "lightweight", "fragrance-free", "dermatologist-tested".
- Disallowed: medical/curative claims, "anti-aging cure", comparative claims vs named competitors.
