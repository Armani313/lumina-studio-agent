# aifreelance.shop listing copy for Lumina

English only (marketplace listing language). The Detailed Description field supports:
**bold**, `## Title` headings, `- item` / `1. item` lists, `[text](url)` links; a blank
line starts a new paragraph.

Numbers must stay in sync with `lumina/pricing.py` (BASE_PRICE/PER_IMAGE/PER_VIDEO/PER_CARD,
FREE_REVISIONS) and `default_spec()` (16 images + 2 videos + 2 cards → $29).

No external links on purpose: off-platform links in a listing read as spammy and lead buyers
away; the live-view link is delivered per order instead.

## One Product Photo In — a Launch-Ready Content Pack Out, in Minutes

Lumina is an autonomous AI product-content studio. Send a single photo of your product and a couple of lines about where you sell — it researches your brand, plans the shoot and delivers a complete, ready-to-publish package: photos, videos, product cards and copy, typically within 15–40 minutes.

## What You Get — Standard Package ($29)

- 📸 **16 photorealistic product images** — hero shots, macro details, lifestyle scenes, flat-lays and clean e-commerce frames, in the right aspect ratios for your platforms (4:5, 1:1, 9:16, 16:9)
- 🎬 **2 short videos** — 360° spin, voiceover ad, UGC-style or macro, sized for Reels, TikTok or Amazon
- 🛍️ **2 designed product cards** with crisp real typography — no garbled AI text
- ✍️ **Marketing copy in your language** — titles, short and long SEO descriptions, bullet points and keywords, with variants for Instagram, TikTok, Amazon, Facebook or your website

Need a different mix? The scope is fully flexible — we agree it in chat before you pay.

## Your Product in Every Shot — Guaranteed by QA

Every image and video is generated from your actual photo, not a lookalike. A built-in multimodal QA reviewer compares each asset against the original product and your brand, scores it, and automatically regenerates anything that drifts — before you ever see it. The quality scorecard ships with the delivery.

## On-Brand, Not Generic

Drop a link to your website or brand guidelines — Lumina reads it live during the chat and matches your palette, tone and style. No link? It researches your product category and proposes a strategy itself.

## Watch It Work — Live

Every order includes a public live link: watch the agent think, plan, generate and QA your assets in real time. No black box.

- **$1** per image

- **$1** per card

The standard package above comes to **$29**; just 8 images and no video is **$16**. You agree the exact scope and price in chat before anything is funded, and payment sits in escrow until you accept the delivery.

## Revisions Included

Every order includes **3 free revisions**. Each one redoes exactly what you ask to change and keeps everything else untouched. Questions are always free.

3. Optionally add a link to your site or brand guidelines, plus any mood or must-haves.

Chat in any language — Lumina replies in yours.

A product launch used to take a photographer, a studio, a designer and a copywriter — and two weeks. **Now it's one message.**
